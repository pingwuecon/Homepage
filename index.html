This is Ping Wu. Welcome to my website.

I am a Lecturer in the Department of Economics at the University of Strathclyde in Scotland.

I am a Research Fellow in the Economic Statistics Centre of Excellence (ESCoE).

My current research agenda focuses on mixed frequency VARs. I am interested in Bayesian econometric methods. Recent work examines: unobserved component models, mixed frequency models, order invariant VARs.
